Privacy Policy — Extynct Studios

Effective Date: October 14, 2025
We do not sell your data

Summary: We collect only what we need to run and improve our games and services, verify ownership, enable online features, and keep things secure. We don’t sell your personal information. For questions or requests, email Glen@evolvedphoenixstudios.com.

1) Information We Collect

a) Information you provide

  • Account/profile data you share with us (e.g., display name, email, support requests).
  • In‑game preferences, settings, or saves you choose to sync to cloud services.
  • Feedback, bug reports, or survey responses.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Gameplay and usage data (session times, progression, items/equipment, match stats, crash logs).
  • Device and technical data (OS version, hardware identifiers, language/locale, IP address, time zone, performance metrics).
  • Diagnostics and anti‑cheat/anti‑fraud signals (where applicable).

c) Information from third‑party platforms

If you connect or access via a third party (e.g., Steam, Epic, PlayStation, Xbox, mobile stores, Discord), we may receive:

  • Platform user ID, display name, avatar.
  • Entitlements (ownership, DLCs, in‑app purchases).
  • Friends list or presence only if you grant permission and the feature requires it.
  • Cloud‑save identifiers or profile metadata supported by that platform.

Examples of vendors/integrations you might use: Steamworks, Unity Services (Authentication, Cloud Save, Analytics, Cloud Diagnostics), Discord Rich Presence, PlayFab/GameAnalytics, Sentry/Backtrace. Replace or remove this line if not applicable.

2) How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Services and your gameplay experience.
  • Provide online/social features (multiplayer, leaderboards, presence, cloud saves).
  • Verify entitlements and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Diagnose crashes and performance issues.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We process data under the bases of legitimate interests, contract, consent (where required), and legal obligations.

3) Sharing of Information

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, crash reporting, support).
  • Platform providers (e.g., Steam/Epic/console/mobile) to verify entitlements and enable platform features.
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers (e.g., merger/acquisition) with comparable safeguards.

We do not sell your personal information.

4) International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses). Contact us for details.

5) Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize the data.

6) Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, object, restrict, withdraw consent, or port your data. To exercise rights, contact Glen@evolvedphoenixstudios.com. We may ask to verify your identity.

7) Cookies & Tracking (Websites Only)

If you visit our websites, we may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve content. You can adjust cookie settings in your browser and (if available) via our cookie banner.

8) Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical measures designed to protect your information (e.g., encryption in transit, access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will promptly delete it from our systems.

If you are in the EEA/UK or other regions with a higher age of digital consent (often under 16), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable age of consent without verifiable parental permission.

Parents/Guardians: If you believe your child has provided personal information to us without your consent, please contact us at Glen@evolvedphoenixstudios.com. We will remove the information and, if applicable, terminate the child’s access.

10) Third‑Party Links

Our Services may link to third‑party sites or services that we do not control. Their practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective Date” and, when required, provide additional notice (e.g., in‑game notice, website banner). Your continued use signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
