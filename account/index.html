Manage your account

Create an account you can use across the community forum. Accounts are saved directly into this website’s repository so they work wherever you sign in.

Create a new account

New accounts are committed to the repository so everyone can sign in from any device.

Sign in

After your account exists in the repository, sign in with your email or username and password. Once signed in you can post on the community forum.

Not signed in.

Heads up: your browser blocked local storage, so your sign-in will reset after you close this tab.

Need to start posting? Head back to the community forum.

How it works

Account details are hashed in your browser and saved to hidden/community/accounts.json within this repository using the GitHub REST API.

Forum sessions are stored locally so the site knows who you are, while passwords never leave the browser unhashed.

When you’re ready to post, visit the Community Forum. Attachments upload into uploads/community/ alongside the site.
